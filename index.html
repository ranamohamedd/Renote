About Us

Renote

Renote is the leading notebook brand in Egypt, offering a wide range of high-quality notebooks that are perfect for students, professionals, and anyone who values the art of note-taking. Our notebooks are designed with the latest technology and crafted with the finest materials to ensure that they are durable, functional, and stylish. We are committed to providing the best quality products at the best prices, so that everyone can have access to top-notch notebooks without breaking the bank.

At Renote, we believe that the act of writing is more than just putting pen to paper - it's a creative process that allows you to express yourself, organize your thoughts, and capture your ideas. That's why we're passionate about creating notebooks that inspire you to be your best self and unleash your creativity.

Our notebooks come in a variety of sizes, colors, and designs to suit every taste and need. Whether you're looking for a classic leather-bound notebook for your daily journaling, a colorful spiral notebook for school, or a sleek and modern notebook for work, Renote has got you covered.

With Renote, you can expect nothing but the best. Our notebooks are made with care and attention to detail, and we stand behind every product we sell. Join us on this journey of discovery and creativity, and experience the joy of writing with Renote.

The Founder

Rana Mohamed

Rana is a recent graduate of the Faculty of Computer and Data Science at Alexandria University, and at just 23 years old, she's already making waves in the world of entrepreneurship.

Rana's love of notebooks inspired her to create Renote, a brand that is quickly becoming known for its high-quality products and affordable prices. As a web development student, Rana has a keen eye for design and a deep understanding of what makes a great product. She's poured her heart and soul into Renote, ensuring that every notebook is crafted with the finest materials and designed to inspire creativity and self-expression.

But what really sets Rana apart is her vision for the future. She's determined to make Renote a global brand that's known for its quality, affordability, and innovation. And with her passion, drive, and entrepreneurial spirit, there's no doubt that she'll succeed.

So if you're looking for a notebook brand that's as unique and inspiring as its founder, look no further than Renote.
